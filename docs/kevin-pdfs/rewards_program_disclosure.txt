Rewards Program Disclosure
Last Updated: March 3, 2026
Paylinq Cash and Universal Reward Tokens (URT) are promotional rewards issued by Paylinq.
These rewards are independent from merchant loyalty programs and are not issued or
guaranteed by merchants or travel providers.
Rewards may remain pending until a merchant confirms a transaction. Rewards may be
adjusted or reversed if a transaction is cancelled, refunded, or determined to violate Paylinq
policies.
Reward values may vary depending on merchant category, promotional campaigns,
membership tier, or affiliate commission structure.
