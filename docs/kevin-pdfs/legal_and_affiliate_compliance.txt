Paylinq Legal & Affiliate Compliance Disclosure
Last Updated: March 3, 2026
1. Affiliate Relationships
Paylinq participates in affiliate marketing and referral programs with various merchants, travel
providers, and service platforms. When users access merchants through Paylinq links, widgets, or
integrations, Paylinq may earn a commission or referral fee.
These commissions are paid by merchants and do not increase the price paid by the consumer.
2. Independent Rewards Program
Paylinq Cash and Universal Reward Tokens (URT) are promotional incentives issued solely by
Paylinq unless explicitly stated otherwise. Merchant partners do not issue or control Paylinq rewards.
3. Price Integrity
Paylinq does not alter merchant pricing, add hidden charges, or manipulate discounts. Affiliate
commissions are paid from merchant marketing budgets.
4. Merchant Loyalty Compatibility
Using Paylinq does not prevent customers from receiving merchant loyalty points, credit card rewards,
or membership benefits.
5. Reward Tracking and Validation
Rewards may initially appear as pending until merchant confirmation. Paylinq reserves the right to
adjust or reverse rewards in cases of cancellations, refunds, fraud, or policy violations.
6. Affiliate Network Compliance
Paylinq strives to comply with the policies of participating affiliate networks and merchant programs.
Paylinq does not encourage artificial transactions, misleading promotions, or prohibited advertising
methods.
7. Brand and Trademark Use
All trademarks and brand names belong to their respective owners. Their appearance on Paylinq does
not imply endorsement or partnership unless explicitly stated.
8. Platform Role
Paylinq operates as a referral and rewards platform. Paylinq is generally not the merchant of record
and does not control product fulfillment, travel reservations, or merchant services.
9. Reward Estimates
Displayed rewards may be estimates until final confirmation by the merchant or affiliate network.
10. Regulatory Commitment
Paylinq aims to comply with applicable advertising, consumer protection, and affiliate marketing
regulations.
