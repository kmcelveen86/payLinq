Travel Rewards
Travel the World. Earn Real Rewards.
With Paylinq, members earn Paylinq Points (PP) when booking travel through our integrated
travel marketplace.
Flights, hotels, car rentals, activities, and travel insurance all earn rewards — automatically.
How It Works
•
Book travel through Paylinq’s travel portal.
•
Complete your trip.
•
Earn Paylinq Points.
•
Convert points into Universal Reward Tokens (URT).
1,000 Paylinq Points = 1 URT
1 URT = $10 value (White Tier)
Rewards are funded through partner marketing agreements.
